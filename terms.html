What SYE Is

SYE is a personal digital wellbeing and screen time management tool for iPhone and iPad. It helps you block distracting apps, pause before opening tracked apps, schedule recurring app blocks, and understand your lifetime screen time. SYE is designed for personal, non-commercial use on your own device.

Screen Time Permission

SYE requires Screen Time (Family Controls) permission to function. This permission is granted through Apple's system prompt when you first open the app and can be revoked at any time in Settings → Screen Time. Revoking this permission will disable SYE's blocking, interruption, scheduling, and screen time reporting features. SYE is not responsible for features becoming unavailable if you revoke this permission.

Limitations of the Service

You acknowledge and understand the following limitations:

  • App blocking is not absolute. SYE uses Apple's Screen Time framework to apply shields to apps. A user can bypass blocks by revoking Screen Time permission in Settings. SYE is a tool to support your intentions, not a parental control system.
  • Timing accuracy. Block timers and usage threshold notifications are accurate to within approximately 1 to 5 minutes. This is a system-level limitation of Apple's DeviceActivity framework and is outside SYE's control.
  • Block Scheduling accuracy. Scheduled blocks may start or end up to 5 minutes from the set time due to Apple's system scheduling behaviour.
  • Screen Time data delays. The Expected Phone Use and Today's Screen Time features rely on Apple's DeviceActivity report. This data can take up to 24 hours to populate and may show blank on first install. SYE has no control over the accuracy or availability of this data.
  • Reinstallation. Reinstalling the app removes all assigned app tokens and preferences. You will need to reassign apps to preset slots after reinstalling. This is a security requirement of Apple's Screen Time framework.
  • Category selections in Block Scheduling. Selecting entire app categories in Block Scheduling may not reliably block all apps within that category. For best results, select individual apps.
  • App token visibility. Previously selected apps cannot be displayed in the Block Schedule edit view due to Apple's privacy framework limitations. Your selections remain active even when not visible in the picker.
  • Device compatibility. SYE requires iOS 16 or later. Some features may behave differently depending on iOS version and device model. Testing on Simulator is not supported for Screen Time features.
