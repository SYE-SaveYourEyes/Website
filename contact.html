Screen Time permission is what allows SYE to block apps. Without it, app blocking and soft interruptions cannot function. SYE requests this permission once during onboarding — you can revoke it at any time from iOS Settings → Screen Time.

A soft interruption is a pause screen that appears when you try to open a blocked app. Instead of hard-blocking the app, it shows you a message and gives you a moment to reconsider. You can still open the app — it is a gentle nudge, not a lock.
